Employee Referral

Employee of any organisation is best people to recommend you for any role. Employers also prefer to hire through employee referral as this not only makes their employees happy but also reduce the cost of hiring. This also creates stickiness and creates a channel of communications in case of any grievances. We connect you with multiple employees of every organisation you apply to setup your Introductory call to pitch your candidature.

  • Connect with Employee of Organisations of your interest
  • Setup call at mutual convenience
  • Brainstorm together
  • Take Relationship ahead
  • Pursue employees to take resume ahead
  • Get connected simultaneously with multiple organisations
